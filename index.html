改革公告：从自习到实战的蜕变

为提供更高价值，卷王联盟正式转型为项目与竞赛导向的技术社区，帮助每位成员积累实战经验，提升就业竞争力。

卷王联盟改革公告

从约自习到项目驱动：为每位成员创造真正的成长与价值

改革背景

从单纯自习到项目导向，我们为什么需要变革？

01

改革背景

亲爱的卷王联盟成员及所有关注我们的伙伴们：

自联盟成立以来，我们一直以"图书馆自习"和"日常学习互助"为主要内容。虽然对部分同学提供了基础支持，但仅仅依靠"互相督促"难以让每个人都获得真正的成长和成果，也难以形成足够的价值与稀缺性，更无法满足真正想"卷"出成果、追求高效率与高产出的同学需求。

与此同时，我们也观察到，许多同学在大三或研究生阶段面临严峻的求职压力，往往缺乏能展示自身能力的项目经历或实践案例，以至于在面试时无法拿出有说服力的作品，从而错失理想的实习或就业机会。

为此，我们决定正式启动改革：在保留"约图书馆"功能的同时，把联盟的重点转向"项目与竞赛"，帮助大家积累高含金量的实战经验、丰富履历、为未来就业和深造做好准备。

改革目标

我们要构建一个怎样的技术社区？

02

改革目标

以项目与竞赛为核心 90%
创造更高的社区价值与影响力 85%
保留原有"约图书馆"功能 40%

以项目与竞赛为核心

  • 鼓励大家参与各类技术、学术、创业或研究项目，并积极参加相关竞赛；
  • 在实践中提升能力、打磨作品，为日后求职或深造提供更具说服力的证据。

创造更高的社区价值与影响力

  • 从"简单自习"升级到"深度协作"，使每位成员都能通过实际项目积累成果，而不再仅止于"坐在一起学习"；
  • 高产出、高执行力的团队氛围将吸引更多优质资源和机会，为同学们的未来发展铺路。

保留原有"约图书馆"功能

  • 我们不会舍弃初衷，依然保留"约自习"的渠道，方便日常学习或备考；
  • 但这将成为辅助功能，联盟真正的核心与"亮点"在于项目和竞赛的合作实践。

什么是卷王标准

我们需要怎样的成员组成？

03

我们需要怎样的"卷王"？

高执行力、高产出意愿、高责任感

  • 希望联盟中的所有成员（包括已在群内与后续加入者）都能积极投入至少一个项目或竞赛，按时完成任务、相互支持。
  • 如果你只想"挂名"或完全不参与产出，可能无法跟上联盟的升级方向。

重视实践与未来规划

  • 很多同学在毕业前夕由于缺少项目经验而陷入困境。
  • 我们希望你能在这里积累实战经验，获取真正能在简历或面试中脱颖而出的"硬实力"成果，避免临近毕业时抱憾。

拥抱挑战、共同成长

  • 真正的"卷"并非盲目拼时间，而是在高效的团队协作中攻关难题、提升自我。
  • 在项目和竞赛过程中，你将学到更多技术知识、团队沟通、领导与管理等核心技能。

动态成员管理

如何保持社区的活力与效率？

04

动态成员管理（淘汰机制）

为维持联盟整体效率和质量，我们将实行一定的筛选与淘汰机制：

最低参与要求

  • 每位成员应当参与至少一个项目或竞赛，并在团队中做出实质贡献；
  • 若长期不活跃、不接受任何任务，或对团队产出毫无帮助，经多次提醒仍无改进，将被视为放弃联盟名额，可能移出核心群组。

尊重个人情况

  • 若你在某个阶段因学业、实习或个人事务暂时无法投入，可与管理员沟通，灵活安排；
  • 不会一刀切，但随着联盟发展壮大，我们会逐步细化并严格执行此项制度。

正向激励与回归通道

  • 表现优秀、贡献突出的成员或团队，将获得资源倾斜、项目推荐、内部荣誉等支持；
  • 如果你暂时退出，日后有意愿重新投入，也欢迎再次申请回归。

开放与多元

构建跨文化的技术社区环境

05

开放与多元

尽管初期多为华人同学，但从未对任何背景或国籍做限制；

今后将逐步采用中英文双语或主要英文进行团队讨论，方便更多同学跨文化交流；

无论专业、语言水平如何，只要你真正想"卷"、想取得成果，都能在这里找到舞台。

接下来的行动

如何实现这一转型？

06

接下来的行动

第一阶段

平台与项目规划

  • 继续保留微信群，用于日常沟通和大文件传输；

  • 近期会公布首批项目及竞赛方向，邀请大家根据兴趣和技能自由组队；

  • 管理团队会对现有成员进行投入意向和时间安排的调研，帮助你找到合适的项目或竞赛队伍；
  • 将根据实际情况进行分组，并在后续公布团队名单及具体分工。

07

结语与号召

Reform Background

Why do we need to evolve from study sessions to project-based community?

01

Reform Background

Dear members and friends of the Juanwang Alliance:

Since our establishment, we have primarily focused on library study sessions and basic academic support. While this has provided some assistance, merely "encouraging each other to study" is not enough to generate significant achievements or uniqueness, nor does it fully meet the needs of those aiming for tangible results and higher efficiency.

At the same time, we've noticed that many students face intense job-hunting pressures during their junior/senior year or graduate studies. They often lack convincing project experience or practical cases to showcase, making it harder to stand out in interviews and secure ideal opportunities.

Hence, we are officially launching a reform: while still retaining "library meetups," we will shift our focus toward "projects and competitions" to help members gain substantial hands-on experience and enhance their resumes, preparing for future careers or further studies.

Reform Objectives

What kind of technical community are we building?

02

Objectives of the Reform

Focus on Projects & Competitions 90%
Creating Greater Community Value 85%
Retaining Study Sessions Support 40%

Centering on Projects & Competitions

  • Encourage everyone to engage in technical, academic, entrepreneurial, or research projects, as well as related competitions;
  • Develop real-world skills and create tangible outcomes that strengthen your CV and interview performance.

Generating Greater Community Value & Impact

  • Evolving from "basic study check-ins" to "in-depth collaboration," so each member can accumulate meaningful achievements instead of merely "studying side by side";
  • A high-output, high-accountability environment will attract more quality resources and open valuable doors for your future.

Retaining the Library Study Feature

  • We will not abandon our original approach of arranging study sessions for day-to-day support;
  • However, this function will serve as an auxiliary, with the real emphasis on project-driven practice and competition-based collaboration.

What Makes a "Go-Getter"

What kind of members are we looking for?

03

The "Go-Getters" We Seek

Strong Execution, High-Output Goals, and Responsibility

  • We hope all current and future members will actively participate in at least one project or competition, completing tasks on time and supporting their teams;
  • If you intend to remain idle or "in name only," our new direction may not align with your expectations.

Focus on Practical Experience & Future Planning

  • Many students realize too late that lacking project experience significantly hinders their job prospects;
  • By engaging here, you can accumulate substantial experience and produce verifiable achievements that stand out on your resume, avoiding last-minute regrets before graduation.

Embrace Challenges & Grow Together

  • True "hardcore effort" (or "juàn") isn't just about logging long study hours; it's about tackling challenging tasks efficiently within a collaborative team.
  • Projects and competitions will help you learn more about technical skills, teamwork, leadership, and communication.

Membership Management

How to maintain community vitality and efficiency?

04

Dynamic Membership Management

To ensure overall efficiency and maintain standards, we will implement certain screening and exit mechanisms:

Minimum Participation Requirements

  • Each member should engage in at least one project or competition, making substantive contributions;
  • Members who remain inactive for an extended period, reject all tasks, or do not contribute to team outputs despite repeated reminders may be removed from the core group.

Respect for Individual Circumstances

  • We understand academic workloads, internships, and personal commitments. Please communicate with the admins if you need flexibility;
  • We won't enforce one-size-fits-all rules immediately, but as the alliance grows, we will refine and strictly implement these regulations.

Positive Reinforcement & Rejoining

  • Those who excel or significantly contribute will receive added resources, recognition, or referral opportunities;
  • If you temporarily withdraw, you're welcome to apply for rejoining once you're ready to fully commit again.

Openness & Diversity

Building a cross-cultural technical community

05

Openness & Diversity

Although we initially had mostly Chinese students due to existing connections, we have never restricted membership based on background or nationality;

Going forward, we plan to incorporate more bilingual (Chinese-English) or English-dominant discussions to accommodate diverse cultural contexts;

Regardless of your major or language level, if you truly want to push your limits and produce results, you can find your stage here.

Upcoming Actions

How will we implement this transformation?

06

Upcoming Actions

Phase 1

Platforms & Project Planning

  • We will continue using WeChat for day-to-day communications and file sharing;
  • Meanwhile, we will organize task allocation and progress tracking on Discord or other management tools, providing a more professional environment for projects and competitions.
Phase 2

Forming Project/Competition Teams

  • We will announce the first batch of projects and competitions soon, allowing members to join teams based on their interests and skill sets;
  • You are also encouraged to propose new projects or recommend worthwhile competitions for group exploration.
Phase 3

Member Survey & Grouping

  • The admin team will survey current members to understand their availability and interests, assisting in team matching;
  • We'll then form working teams accordingly and share the details of each team's objectives and responsibilities.

Conclusion & Call to Action

Creating a new era for Juanwang Alliance together

07

Conclusion & Call to Action

We firmly believe that this "reform and upgrade" will provide a more valuable platform for those who genuinely want to excel, ensuring you won't face empty resumes or regrets when it's time to seek employment or graduate opportunities. We invite every alliance member to find like-minded teammates here and push forward together—truly "raising the bar" and achieving tangible outcomes!

For any questions or suggestions regarding this reform, feel free to reach out to the admin team on WeChat or Discord.
